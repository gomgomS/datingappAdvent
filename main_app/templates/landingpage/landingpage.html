About comes.id

comes.id is a modern dating and friendship platform specifically designed for young Adventist Christians. We understand the importance of finding connections within your faith community.

Whether you're looking for friendship, companionship, or a potential life partner, comes.id provides a safe and welcoming environment where you can meet like-minded individuals who share your values and beliefs.

Our mission is to help young Adventists build meaningful relationships that honor God and strengthen the community. Join thousands of young people who have already found their perfect match through comes.id.
